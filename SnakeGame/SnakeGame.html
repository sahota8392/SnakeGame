Snake Game

INSTRUCTIONS [-]

  1. Up, Down, Left, Right move the 'red' snake.
  2. When you 'eat' the white square, your snake will grow by 1 square
  3. Reaching the edge of the square results in the game over
  4. Speed of the snake will increase as your snake grows and your score increases
  5. Click the Restart Game button below to restart
